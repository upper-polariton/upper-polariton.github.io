IV. Performing a QM/MM simulation of the system with Gromacs

QM/MM MD simulation

We now perform a short 1ps QM/MM (AM1/Amber99) equilibration simulation of the system.

For this part, we need a special mdrun binary that has been linked with the mopac library. You can download this binary here mdrun.

To make the mdrun binary executable, use

localhost:~>chmod +x mdrun

We keep using the normal grompp version.

localhost:~>grompp -f qmmm1.mdp -p qmmm.top -n qmmm.ndx -c qmmm1.gro

localhost:~>mdrun -v -c qmmm1out.gro

In the next step we will use the structure after 1 ps to investigate the effect of an excess electron on the stability of the thymine dimer.
