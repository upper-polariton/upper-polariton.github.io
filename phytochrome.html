Phytochrome

Billiverdin force field parameters

We have built a Amber03-compatible force field for the Billiverdin co-factor, which acts as the chromophore in bacteriophytochrome. If you use these parameters in your work, please include a citation to our paper, in which the force field parameterization is described. Thanks. The parameters for use with the Gromacs suite of programs can be downloaded here: phytochrome_ff.tar.gz
